Brief Introduction

According to the previous problem statement which has been demonstrated as:

"How might we make a college student who wants to participate in film festivals (who) address the problem of collecting incomplete and distributed information on several different platforms, meanwhile, cannot find a genuine channel to meet with potential working partners and industry professionals (what) so that she/he can save time and have a better film festival participating experience (why)?"

The later developed journey map should be:
1) Find a film festival
2) Look into a film festival
3) Decide on participating in the film festival
4) Find working partners
5) Shoot and edit footages
6) Examine the finished film
7) Submit the finished film

The scope of the future journey and story mapping exercise will be restricted to #1, #2 and #4 steps, by which it highlights the preparing stage of the entire process, mostly information-seeking and communication-establishing.

In addition, the solution aims to monetize the participation experience by developing a community platform business model, connecting the passionate students with the other participants or professionals who share the same goal and can be mutually beneficial.
